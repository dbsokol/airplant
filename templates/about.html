Shipping Information

We ship anywhere in the United States

Once an order is placed, we will verify your order details, select a healthy plant from our nursery and carefully package them safely in its box. The Company

Born in California, raised wherever you are.

With its Nursery in Southern California, Airplant Gardens grows and ships hundreds of tillandsia and bromeliadae species. Our team has a deeply rooted passion for integrating the outdoors into our homes, and we love sharing our airplants with folks in all corners of the country!

Organic. Plastic-free. Earth-conscious.

Our airplants grow without soil, fertilizers or enhancers. They grow in the air. We take pride in growing these exotic and unique little plants. We believe it's long overdue and vitally important for businesses to operate with our earth and future generations in mind, which is why we use 100% plastic-free packaging. We've made it our mission to walk this earth more gently and do our part for a brighter, greener future.
